How it works

↑ For Givers

  1. 1
    List it in 2 minutes

    Add a photo, pick a category, set pickup window. Your item is live to nearby receivers instantly.

  2. 2
    Get matched nearby

    Receivers who are serious pay ₹9 to commit. You see their karma score and pick the best match.

  3. 3
    Hand it over with an OTP

    When they arrive, they enter a one-time pickup code. Exchange confirmed. Done.

⇄

↓ For Receivers

  1. 1
    Browse what's near you

    See items available within your area. Filter by category. No account needed to look around.

  2. 2
    Commit with ₹9

    A small fee that tells the giver you're serious. Not a charge to keep, just proof you'll show up.

  3. 3
    Pick it up. Enter the OTP.

    Show up at the agreed time, enter the code, take it home. Your karma score goes up instantly.

How we keep it honest

Your kindness deserves a guarantee.

Giving should work. So we made sure it does.

No more "they said you but never showed."
01 ₹

₹9 Receiver Commitment

Anyone claiming an item pays ₹9 first. Real intent to follow through, or pay to filter out the people who never show.

Via Razorpay
02 🔑

OTP Pickup Code

Every exchange gets a one-time code. The giver confirms it when the receiver arrives. No code, no confirmation.

Tamper-proof
03 ★

Karma Score

Every successful exchange builds your score. Reliable people rise to the top. Bad actors lose karma and visibility fast.
